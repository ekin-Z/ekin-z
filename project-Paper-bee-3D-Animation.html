As a designer, I've experienced firsthand the frustrations that can arise during the creative process. This video is a lighthearted nod to those moments when ideas seem to vanish into thin air.
Watch as a crumpled paper dropped by someone annoyed, only to discover that this paper holds the key to bringing up brilliant new things. It's a reminder that even the most seemingly discarded ideas can lead to extraordinary results.
This video is dedicated to all creatives, encouraging them to persevere and celebrate the joy of the creative process. Happy New Year!
