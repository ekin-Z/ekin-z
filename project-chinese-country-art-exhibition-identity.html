A Fresh Perspective on Chinese Country Art.
When discussing Chinese country art, we often focus on its raw, unsophisticated aspects. However, upon closer examination, a harmonious order emerges from seemingly chaotic patterns. The ordinary, everyday life of these artists becomes a source of inspiration, transformed into beautiful and meaningful works of art.
What truly captivates me is the underlying philosophy of these artists: no obscure discourse, their vocation is to elevate the mundane and make everyday life appear extraordinary. To reflect this, I've created a graphic system that utilizes vibrant colors, intentionally avoiding a depiction of the artists' modest living environment. Instead, the focus is on highlighting their humble artistic vision and the beauty they find in the simple things.
