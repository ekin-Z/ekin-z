A Countdown to Olympic Glory.
Orange presents a dynamic animated greeting for the 2024 Olympics. The Orange mini logo becomes a versatile athlete, showcasing athletic feats. The animation captures the spirit of competition, achievement, and victory.
Visual references to Olympic disciplines like javelin, athletics, weightlifting, diving, and skateboarding are included. A suspenseful soundtrack adds to the captivating experience. This animation celebrates the anticipation of the Olympic Games.
