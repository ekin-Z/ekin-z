As Orange prepares to launch its 5G network, the brand seeks a new identity that captures the excitement and innovation of this groundbreaking technology while remaining true to its core values. The ideal identity should be flexible, adaptable, and infused with a sense of joy and optimism. It should seamlessly integrate with Orange's existing brand code, ensuring a cohesive and recognizable experience for customers.
We successfully addressed this challenge by developing a new brand identity that perfectly aligns with Orange's vision for 5G. The client was impressed with the outcome and commended our team for their creativity and expertise.
